# Biblia Traditio — Beta (TestFlight notes)

Thank you for helping test **Biblia Traditio**, a Catholic study Bible that lets
you read Scripture *with the Church*: the commentary of the Church Fathers, one
tap from any verse, fully offline.

## What to test

- **Reading.** Open the Bible, move between books, chapters and verses, read in
  Portuguese.
- **The commentary-first verse panel.** Tap a verse → the *Padres da Igreja*
  (Church Fathers) tab opens first. A small gold dot marks verses that have
  commentary. **This is the heart of the app — tell us how it feels.**
- **Parallel Reading.** Turn it on from the reader (the columns icon) and pair
  Portuguese with the Latin Vulgate, verse by verse.
- **Your marks.** Notes, highlights, favorites, bookmarks, and sharing a verse.
- **Offline packages.** Install and remove the Church Fathers' commentary in
  Settings → Offline resources.
- **Liturgy tab** and overall navigation, calm and clarity.

## Known limitations

- The **Portuguese Bible is a beta/internal text** — its source and translation
  are still being confirmed before any public release. Wording may change.
- The **Church Fathers' commentary** includes machine-translated/adapted text
  and may contain inaccuracies. Read it with discernment.
- **Daily Mass readings (Lectionary) are not included yet.** The liturgical
  calendar works; the readings show a "coming soon" notice.
- In **Parallel Reading**, some Psalm or verse numbering may differ slightly
  between the Portuguese and the Latin.
