# Privacy Policy — Biblia Traditio

**Last updated: 16 June 2026**

Biblia Traditio ("the app", "we", "us") is a Catholic study Bible built to be
private by default. This policy explains what the app does with data. In short:
the app works locally on your device, requires no account, and does not track
you.

## What data we collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers. Biblia Traditio has no account
system, no login, and no user profiles. We do not run analytics or advertising,
and we do not sell or share your data — because we do not collect it.

## Local app data

Your notes, highlights, favorites, bookmarks, reading progress, and settings are
stored only on your device. This data stays on your device unless you choose to
share a specific verse (for example, through the system share sheet) or unless
it is included in a backup you make through your own device or operating system
(such as iCloud or Google backup, governed by their policies). You can remove
all of this data at any time by deleting the app.

## Optional contact via WhatsApp

If you choose to contact us, the app can open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message
to our number. This only happens when you tap the contact option. Any message
you send is handled by WhatsApp under WhatsApp's own privacy policy, and by us
only to reply to you. Contacting us is entirely optional.

## Offline content packages

The app may download optional offline content packages — for example, the Church
Fathers' commentary. These are read-only content files delivered as static
downloads. Downloading them does not require an account and does not transmit
personal information about you beyond the ordinary network request needed to
fetch the file.

## Third-party services

- **WhatsApp** — only if you choose to contact us (see above), under WhatsApp's
  own privacy policy.
- **Your app store and OS backup** — governed by Apple's and Google's policies.

The app does **not** currently include analytics, crash reporting, advertising,
or tracking SDKs. The app does **not** request location, camera, microphone,
contacts, or tracking permissions.

## Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. Because it collects no personal data and
requires no account, it does not knowingly collect personal information from
anyone, including children, and does not build profiles of users of any age.

## Changes to this policy

If our data practices change — for example, if we later add optional accounts,
cloud sync, analytics, or crash reporting — we will update this policy, revise
the "Last updated" date, and, where the law requires it, ask for your consent.
Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
